Founder and Lead Developer

Shoppie is a price tracking software that allows users to save items on eccommerce stores and track their prices. Users are then alerted via email when an item's price drops below their desired price.


Shoppie is built using React, TypScript, Express and MySQL and is currently only hosted locally. Full hosting and deployment will be rolled out over the following month.
